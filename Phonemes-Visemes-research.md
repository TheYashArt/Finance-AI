# Phoneme Visual Categories

## Vowel Categories

| Category | Phonemes | Example Words | Mouth Shape | Teeth Shape | Tongue Position |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Low Open (AA/AE/AH/AO) | AA, AE, AH, AO | Balm, Cat, Buck, Caught, Odd, At, Hut, Ought | Wide open jaw (lowered 50-80%), lips neutral/stretched | Teeth apart, mostly hidden | Low/front to mid (pushed down/back) |
| Diphthong Rise (AY/EY) | AY, EY | Bite, Bait, Hide, Ate | Jaw medium open, transitioning wide/narrow | Teeth slightly visible | Low front rising to high front |
| Front High (IH/IY/EH) | IH, IY, EH | Bit, Beat, Bet, It, Eat, Ed | Lips spread horizontally (smile-like), jaw slightly open | Top teeth visible on smile | High front, arched up |
| R-colored (ER) | ER | Bird, Hurt | Medium open, lips slightly rounded | Teeth apart | Mid/curled back (rhotics) |
| Back Round (OW/UH/UW/OY) | OW, OY, UH, UW | Boat, Boy, Book, Boot, Oat, Toy, Hood, Two | Lips rounded/pouted forward, jaw low-medium | Teeth hidden | High/mid back, pulled back/rounded |
| Back Low (AW) | AW | Bout, Cow | Jaw open wide, lips rounded/slightly protruded | Teeth apart | Low back gliding |

| Lips_Protrude     | G, K, NG (velars subtle)             | Slight forward push, open interior.                                 |
